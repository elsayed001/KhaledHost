Guidelines

Follow these guidelines to effectively use the Vulnerability Simulator.

Simulation Rules & Best Practices

- Always follow ethical hacking principles.
- Do not use simulation techniques on real-world systems without permission.
- Respect privacy and data integrity.

- Navigate to the Challenges section.
- Choose a challenge set and follow the instructions.
- Use the built-in terminal for testing commands.

- The assessment section evaluates your knowledge.
- Select a test, answer the multiple-choice questions.
- Earn points based on correct answers.

- If you encounter a bug, use the Contact page.
- Provide a detailed description of the issue.
- Our support team will address your concerns promptly.

- Use knowledge responsibly and legally.
- Never exploit vulnerabilities in unauthorized environments.
- Encourage a secure and ethical hacking community.
